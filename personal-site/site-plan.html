Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site will to be able to look at the events that are available for the customers and fans and they can click on that event, sign up for it, and the site creater can look at all of the people that have signed up and what they signed up for.

Audience

The audience will be fans of StarWars and people that love to do activity events such as lightsaber battles, costume contests, and even blaster practice.

Dynamic elements

Dynamic elements that will be used with JavaScript in this site will be the signing up for the events and the information will be saved and be able to be recalled to be seen by the site user.
